On the other hand, I found that the gameplay was a little on the easy side. Sure, I could have played the game on hard mode, but I feel that the normal mode should have provided more of a challenge because it was generally quite easy to get through. The greatest difficulty in Bioshock was having enough ammo to get by, often I ran out but that may be because I'm a bad shot. They really improved the ammo management in the second game however, which was great. Also, the ending was a little anticlimatic, I was expecting some sort of big interactive ending that really went out with a bang, but there was just a little narrative at the end. The ending was good, but it could have been much better.
